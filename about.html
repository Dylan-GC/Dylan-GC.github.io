About Us

We aim to to educate, inform and inspire our visitors with the stories of Grantham and the surrounding areas. Bringing our collective heritage to life through physical and digital exhibitions and experiences, Grantham Museum is for and by the people of Grantham.


Who We Are

In early 2011 the Grantham Community Heritage Association (GCHA) was formed by a group of local volunteers to manage Grantham Museum after it was closed. The GCHA worked towards re-opening the Museum in time to celebrate the Queen's Jubilee, and the Museum has been open ever since.
